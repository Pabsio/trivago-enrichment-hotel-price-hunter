Hotel Price Hunter

Find the best hotel deals, select them, and copy HTML snippets straight into Contentful.

01 · Introduction

What is Hotel Price Hunter?

Hotel Price Hunter is an internal tool for the HolidayPirates editorial team. It connects to Google Sheets where the enrichment agent deposits hotel data from Trivago, and lets you filter, sort, select and copy HTML snippets ready to paste into Contentful.

In one sentence: you find the best hotel dates, verify the price, and copy the snippet — all in one place.

How to open it

The tool is live at trivago-enrichment.netlify.app — open it directly in your browser, no setup needed.

ℹ️ The tool connects to Google Sheets automatically on load. If data doesn't appear, check your internet connection and try the ↺ Refresh button.
02 · Quick start

Your first selection in 60 seconds

Open the tool — go to trivago-enrichment.netlify.app. Hotels for ES load automatically.
Pick your market — click a market chip (🇪🇸 ES, 🇩🇪 DE, 🇬🇧 UK…). The table reloads with that market's data.
Select a hotel — click a hotel chip to filter all dates for that property.
Apply filters — narrow down by month, check-in day, nights or price per night.
Select deals — check the boxes on the rows you want, or click ☰ Top 20 cheapest.
Copy snippets — click ↓ Copy selected and paste into Contentful.

04 · Hotels

Market selection

Click a market chip to load hotel data for that market. ES loads automatically when you open the tool. Data is cached per session — switching between markets is instant once loaded. To get fresh data from the sheet, click ↺ Refresh.

ℹ️ Prices extracted from Google Hotels — used as a reference to identify the cheapest check-in dates. When you check the price on Trivago it may turn out to be even cheaper.

Filters

Hotel

Select a specific hotel from the chips to see only its dates. Select All to show dates across all hotels in the market.

Month & Check-in day

Both support multi-selection — click individual chips to toggle them on or off. Clicking All resets the selection.

Nights

Double-handle slider. Drag the handles or type values in the number inputs — both controls stay in sync.

Max price per night

Single-handle slider. The filter applies to the Google per-night price. Shows No limit at maximum.

Sorting

Click any column header to sort; click again to reverse. Sortable columns: Check-in, Nights, ~Price/night, ~Total, TV/night, TV Total.

Copying snippets to Contentful

Check the box on each row you want. Selected rows highlight in purple.
Or click ☰ Top 20 cheapest to automatically select the 20 cheapest dates.
Click ↓ Copy N snippets. All HTML snippets are copied to your clipboard.
Paste directly into Contentful.

When a Trivago price is available, the snippet includes it automatically:

<p><strong>11.06 - 14.06 - 59€</strong> <a href="https://www.trivago.es/..." target="_blank" data-track-source="trivago_es_accommodations_affiliate"></a></p>

If no Trivago price is available yet, the price is omitted from the snippet.

To clear your selection, click ✕ Deselect all.

ℹ️ Always verify the price via the Check ↗ link on Trivago before publishing.
05 · Run Agent

Launching the Hotel Enrichment Agent

The ⚙️ Run Agent tab opens the n8n enrichment agent form in a popup window. The form has two steps — no need to open n8n manually.

Click the ⚙️ Run Agent tab — the form popup opens automatically.
Step 1: Type the hotel name. n8n searches and generates the available options.
Step 2: Select the correct hotel from the dropdown and click Submit.
The agent runs in the background — check the Google Sheet in a few minutes for results.
ℹ️ If the popup is blocked by your browser, click ⚡ Abrir formulario del agente on the Run Agent tab to open it manually. Allow popups for this site once and it will open automatically from then on.
06 · Reference

Workflow tips

Recommended workflow

  1. Select your market.
  2. Click a hotel chip to focus on one property.
  3. Apply month and day-of-week filters to match your editorial calendar.
  4. Click ☰ Top 20 cheapest to pre-select the best dates.
  5. Check TV/night column — rows with a Trivago price are ready to publish.
  6. Verify prices via Check ↗ before finalising.
  7. Click ↓ Copy selected and paste into Contentful.

Refresh & cache

Data is cached per market during the session for fast switching. If the Google Sheet has been updated, click ↺ Refresh (top right) to reload all data fresh from the sheet — cache-busting is built in so you always get the latest data.

Price context

  • ~Price/night and ~Total are per-night approximations from Google Hotels.
  • TV/night and TV Total are verified prices scraped directly from Trivago.
  • The Trivago price is the one that goes into the published snippet.
  • Always verify via Check ↗ before publishing.
